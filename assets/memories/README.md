# Generated memory scenes

These seven project-bound illustrations were created with the built-in `imagegen` tool on 2026-07-17. `References/Interface.png` and `References/Gameplay.png` anchored every prompt so the scenes share the game's painterly deep-violet, antique-gold, muted-rose, and cream palette. All prompts requested a wide cinematic composition without text, logos, UI, or watermarks.

- `raincoat.png`: A wealthy woman in a storm-soaked museum threshold, doubled in the glass beside a child-sized yellow raincoat.
- `teacup.png`: Elian and Mara arguing across a service table while a cracked strawberry teacup trembles between them.
- `umbrella.png`: A dry gloved hand planting a wet silver umbrella beside Celeste's belongings near the archive corridor.
- `elevator.png`: Mara pressing a concealed brass control while Jonah watches from the landing and gold archive dust catches the light.
- `musicbox.png`: Four visible waves of melody traveling through a corridor of turning locks toward the awakened Glass Orchard.
- `guestbook.png`: A docent concealing a consent clause while a visitor signs a velvet guestbook over a damning carbon copy.
- `orchard.png`: Mara playing the archive phrase as the glass tree draws Elian's living reflection into its luminous fruit.
